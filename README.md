**TripMindAI** helps you plan smarter trips, under budget. The platform combines:
- **AI Itinerary Generation** (Groq LLM) - Creates realistic, budget-aware day-by-day plans
- **Receipt Scanning** (Groq Vision) - Photographs receipts to auto-log expenses
- **Live Budget Dashboard** - Real-time expense tracking and runway calculations
- **Packing Assistant** - AI-powered personalized packing checklists
- **Weather Integration** - 7-day forecasts for your destination
